Title
Multicast Group Membership Discovery MIB

Applicability

This document defines a portion of the Management Information Base (MIB) for use with network management protocols in Internet Protocol Version 6 internets. In particular, it describes objects used for managing the Internet Group Management Protocol (IGMP) and the Multicast Listener Discovery (MLD) protocol.
