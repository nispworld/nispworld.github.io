Title
Specifications Defining the Joint Dismounted Soldier System Interoperability Network (JDSSIN) - Security

Applicability

The standard on Dismounted Soldier Systems (DSS) Standards and Protocols for Command, Control, Communications and Computers (C4) Interoperability (DSS C4 Interoperability) aims at enabling interoperability through a standardized exchange of information between C4 systems used by dismounted soldiers across NATO or Partners for Peace (PfP) force boundaries.

The DSS C4 Interoperability solution contains

  • A Joint Dismounted Soldier System (JDSS) Gateway, acting as a message translator, added to each C4 sub-system of a national DSS consisting of

  • Joint Dismounted Soldier System Data Model (JDSSDM)

  • Joint Dismounted Soldier Information Exchange Mechanism (JDSSIEM) o User Datagram Protocol (UDP)

  • Internet Protocol (IP)

  • Ethernet

  • A physical connection between the JDSS Gateway and the Loaned Radio based on STANAG 4619.

  • A Loaned Radio.
