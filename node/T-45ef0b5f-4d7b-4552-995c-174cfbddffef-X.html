Title
Cyberspace Operations Preparation
Description

Preparation for Cyberspace Operations consists of two processes * ‘Pre-deployment training ‘. Planning and exercising the integration of COs should take place before deployment. Ideally, this involves all elements of the force. * ‘Preparing the joint operations area. ‘ Shaping, securing and maintaining access to the joint operations area is a pre-condition for mission success and is coordinated with the commander. COs may support such activities. The commander works to understand and assess COs that are used to shape the environment, to ensure they are legally and operationally acceptable, and within policy constraints
