Title
Test
Description

The Test and Evaluation Process is an iterative five-step process that provide objective evidence for decision makers at various times during capability development. The Test process begins during the formative stages of a capability development programme with the Coordination Function for V&V and Testing, in which the information needs of the various decision makers are formulated in conjunction with the development of the programme requirements, acquisition strategy, and Analysis of Alternatives.
