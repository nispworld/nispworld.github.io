Title
Organisational Design and Change of Military Manpower
Description

The roles and responsibilities for Alliance Military Bodies are defined by the Military Committee. Manpower staffs in the International Military Staff and in the Peacetime Establishment Authorities(PEA) then develop organisational structures based on these roles and responsibilities. Each PEA is responsible for maintaining a matrix of roles and responsibilities showing the assignment of each by functional group.
