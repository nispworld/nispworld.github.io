Title
Command and Control Operations
Description
The ‘Command and Control (C2) Processes ‘ are composed of a collection of business processes that are implemented and executed to support the execution of military operations in all domains Land, Maritime, Air, Cyberspace and Space. The five sub-processes of C2 can be grouped into the inter-connectivity and synchronization of three interlocking phases collecting (sensing and processing data), decision-making (sense-making and deciding), and effecting (acting and assessing). The overarching piece of C2 is connecting, as it enables and links all other processes. [[File C2_Phases.png center 400px Thumb C2 Cycle]]
