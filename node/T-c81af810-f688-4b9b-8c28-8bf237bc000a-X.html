Title
Maritime Information Management Applications
Description

Maritime Information Management Applications enable users to access, process and disseminate information from external sources (e.g. commercially available databases, Internet sites) that present a specific interest for the maritime community of interest, and to define, produce and maintain information about maritime objects and entities.
