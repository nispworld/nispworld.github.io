Title
Land Operations Execution Monitoring Process
Description
Monitoring the current situation and progress of the operation process take different forms during Planning, Preparation, and Execution phases of the Operations Cycle. Land Operations Execution Monitoring processes are a subset of [[Assessing Operations Assessment processes]], and allow commanders to assess the situation in terms of progress of the operation.
