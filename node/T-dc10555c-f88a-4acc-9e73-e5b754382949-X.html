Title
Identify Capability Surpluses
Description

There are two types of gaps shortages and surpluses. A shortage occurs when current or planned capabilities fall short of identified requirements and certain requirements can not be fulfilled. When existing or planned capabilities exceed the identified requirements, there is a capability surplus for which a decommissioning process is initiated.
