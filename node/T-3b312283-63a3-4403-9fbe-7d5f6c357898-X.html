Title
CIS Security Applications
Description

The CIS Security Applications enable users to create and maintain a secure environment that meets the security objectives of Communications and Information Systems (CIS) to handle all information. The CIS Security Applications aim to ensure the confidentiality of information by controlling the disclosure of, and access to information, supporting systems, services and resources; the integrity and availability of information, supporting systems, services and resources; the reliable identification and authentication of persons, devices and services accessing CIS; and the appropriate non-repudiation for individuals and entities having processed the information.
