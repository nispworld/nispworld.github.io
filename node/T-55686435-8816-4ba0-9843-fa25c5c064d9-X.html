Title
Recognized Cyber Picture Application
Description

The Recognized Cyber Picture (RCP) Application enables users to access, compile, generate and share the correlated and fused cyber picture in order to provide enhanced situational awareness of the cyber domain, including on-going activities and their relationships. The RCP provides a near-real-time logical presentation of processed, all-source cyber data (current and planned).
