Title
Land OPLAN and OPORDER Development Process
Description

The Land Operational Plan (OPLAN) and Operational Order (OPORDER) Development Process is Phase 4b of the Land C2 Processes at the level of the Land Component Command (LCC). The purpose of the Land OPLAN and OPORDER Development Process is to * Develop the arrangements and further specify the required activities to implement the concept of operations; * Specify the conduct of land operations, including the deployment, employment and sustainment of forces; and * Provide a basis for planning by subordinate/supporting land forces and entities and subsequent adaptation, as required, to meet changes in the land operational environment.
