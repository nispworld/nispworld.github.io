Title
Manage Manpower Post Categories, the Manpower Mix and Manning Responsibilities
Description

The capabilities and cost effectiveness of any Alliance Military Body are significantly influenced by the sum and mix of all Alliance employed manpower resources available to perform the required work (comprising authorized international military and civilian posts, local wage rate civilian employees, contractor personnel, and consultant personnel). Obtaining the proper balance of these categories of manpower resources is a primary responsibility of all Alliance Commanders and their manpower management staffs. This process deals with establishing various manpower categories and determining the most efficient mix, as well as clarifying Peacetime Establishment manning responsibilities.
