Title
Land RFI Management Application
Description

The Land Request for Information (RFI) Management Application enables users to support the management and tracking of Requests for Information received from external sources. Functionality is provided to log the receipt of RFIs, assign these to the appropriate response element in the command, track the compilation and review of the response, and issue approved responses.
