Title
Mortuary Affairs Application
Description

The Mortuary Affairs Application enables users to plan and support all activities for search, recovery, tentative identification (ID), evacuation and, when required, temporary interment, disinterment, decontamination, and reinterment of deceased military personnel, noncombatants, and allied, coalition partner, third country, and adversary personnel. This application includes functions to facilitate the operation of mortuary affairs processing points; the preparation and coordination of the shipment of human remains; maintenance of temporary interment sites including the recording of interment and disinterment actions; collection, inventory, storage and processing of personal effects of deceased; and compilation of records and other data to support search and recovery (S&R) of human remains.
