Title
Logistics Collaborative Planning Application
Description

The Logistics Collaborative Planning (LCP) Application enables operators to undertake collaborative planning within the context of a specific operation in order to establish logistic support requirements, identify potential logistic solutions and select the appropriate solution in time for them to be implemented as early as possible. This includes Logistics Force Generation. This functionality provides guidance to all other support and logistics domains (e.g. Medical, Supply, Movement and Transportation) in order to elaborate their specific planning and ensures consistency of all the specific plans.
