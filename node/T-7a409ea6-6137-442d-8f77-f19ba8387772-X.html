Title
Tactical Messaging Access Services
Description
The Tactical Messaging Access Services provide the delivery or exchange of Tactical Data Link (TDL)-formatted messages, over a man-machine interface (e.g. a keyboard) or machine-machine interface (e.g. an avionics two-wire data bus). The physical layer of the Tactical Data Links is covered under Transmission Services (Air-Ground-Air, and Maritime Surface-Surface).
