Title
Maritime Logistics and Support Application
Description

Maritime logistic support includes the provision of the stores and spare parts required by units, the supply and resupply of fuel and lubricants, ammunition and food, and the provision of medical support, maintenance support, personnel support and hotel services. Logistics, focused on total asset visibility and in-stride sustainment, will leverage the network to monitor stocks of critical supplies, determine when essential parts are nearing failure, track assets, and plan and execute deliveries. The Maritime Logistics and Support Application allows the user to manage the information on Logistic Sites, either afloat or ashore.
