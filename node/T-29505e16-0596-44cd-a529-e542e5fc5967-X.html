Title
CTE Capabilities Modelling Application
Description

The Collective Training and Exercises (CTE) Capabilities Modelling Application enables users to develop and manage prototypes of military capabilities in order to provide a coherent representation of units, entities and their organic systems across all relevant applications involved in group training and exercises.
