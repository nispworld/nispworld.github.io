Title
AMD Friendly Capabilities and Interoperability Analysis Process
Description

The Air and Missile Defence (AMD) Friendly Capabilities and Interoperability Analysis Process is used to analyze Host Nation and joint/coalition/allied air defence assets to determine operational capabilities and interoperability. It involves analysis of the higher headquarters mission, JFC guidance, available resources, assumptions and risk. Intel products support further analysis of the friendly situation as well as the enemy situation. This process generate a mission analysis briefing providimg an overview of the analysis to date and a mission statement to the JFACC/ADC.
