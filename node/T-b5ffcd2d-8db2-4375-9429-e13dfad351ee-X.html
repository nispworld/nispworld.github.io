Title
Land Situation Awareness Process
Description

The purpose of the Land Situation Awareness Process, supported by Knowledge Development, is the development and maintenance of a level of understanding of land situation to support assessments, and decision-making in the provision of Land advice to Joint Force Command (JFC) during the planning and conduct of operations. Many of the processes mirror those found in the C2 for Operations [[Processing]] section.
