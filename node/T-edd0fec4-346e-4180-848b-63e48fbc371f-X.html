Title
CTE Analysis Applications
Description

The Collective Training and Exercises (CTE) Analysis Applications enable users to collect data and prepare, conduct, document and brief analysis of group training and exercises in order to increase the learning effect for both the training audience and the Education, Training, Exercises and Evaluation (ETEE) Community of Interest (COI).
