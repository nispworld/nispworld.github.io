Title
Maritime Surface Warfare Application
Description

The Maritime Surface Warfare Application enables users to support search and rescue and anti-surface warfare in determining force levels, shipboard, and related support requirements, and major characteristics of programs involving cruiser-destroyer, combat logistics and auxiliary ships (less submarine tenders).
