Title
AMD Planning Processes
Description

The Air and Missile Defence (AMD) Planning Processes are responsible for developing plans for defensive operations to counter air and missile threats. These plans aim to establish air and missile defence identification procedures and to effectively integrate air defence capabilities of component forces, Host Nations (HN) and coalition forces, as applicable. The Joint Forces Commander (JFC) operational concept and apportionment decisions serve as the basis for these plans.
