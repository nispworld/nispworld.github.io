Title
Risk Management Governance Processes
Description
The processes for Risk Management (RM) Governance describe the activities to govern an organization’s risk management framework that is focussed on the definition, maintenance and continual improvement of an organization’s RM processes. The [[Risk Management Processes]] are about the execution of the RM framework in order to support a risk-aware decision making process.
