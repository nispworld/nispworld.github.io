Title
JISR Processing Process
Description

The Joint Intelligence, Surveillance and Reconnaissance (JISR) Processing Process supports the conversion of collected data to a useable format. For example, SIGINT intercepts are translated, HUMINT reports are formatted and source information added, and IMINT is converted to customer specifications.
