Title
Terminate Operations
Description
The Processes to support a commander when the objectives have been achieved, in order to preserve what has been gained, and to ensure it endures. It is the strategic commander’s responsibility to make the appropriate examination and to determine termination criteria which describe military and non-military conditions that justify the recommendation to terminate operations.
