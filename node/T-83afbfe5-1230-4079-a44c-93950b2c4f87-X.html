Title
AMD Air Defence Plan Components Coordination Process
Description

The Air and Missile Defence (AMD) Air Defence Plan (ADP) Components Coordination Process is employed by Command and Control (C2) Planners in order to compile and coordinate the draft ADP with component liaisons, Host Nation, Allied and Partner Nation air and missile defence commands, Space liaisons, agency and other stakeholders. The draft ADP will be submitted to the Air Defence Commander (ADC) for review.
