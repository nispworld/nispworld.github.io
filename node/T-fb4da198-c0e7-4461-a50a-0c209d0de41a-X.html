Title
APOD/APOE Status and Selection Application
Description

The Aerial Port of Debarkation (APOD) / Aerial Port of Embarkation (APOE) Status and Selection Application enables users to process airfield information and to determine suitability for mobility operations, such as runway information, service support, airfield equipment, parking areas, load limits, notes, hazards and other restrictions pertaining to airport operations.
