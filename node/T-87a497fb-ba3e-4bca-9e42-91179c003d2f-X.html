Title
NRF Readiness Reporting Application
Description

The NATO Response Force (NRF) Readiness Reporting Application support NRF Readiness Reporting Process by managing the readiness and status reporting and assessment of the forces committed to each NRF rotation and used by all forcess assigned to the NRF. The process is tightly integrated with Operations Planning, ORBAT Management and Force Generations and divided into three distinct phases Phases I and II consist of the establishment of the reporting framework for each NRF rotation between the ACO and force contributing nations with the flow of information commencing at the Strategic Level. Once the reporting framework is in place (Phase III), the tactical level generates all readiness information and reports it up the NRF chain of command.
