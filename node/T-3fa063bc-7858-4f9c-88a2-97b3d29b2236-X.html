Title
Budget Management Application
Description

The Budget Management Application enables users to provide support, management and follow up control for the planning, budgeting, forecasting, monitoring and analysis of NATO operations from the financial perspective. This function supports in particular the production and management of documents such as Medium Term Financial Plans (MTFP), Budget Submission Figures (BSF). This function also supports the management of Budgets (in particular the delegation, and authorisation of transfer, of (yearly) budgets, the freezing of budget lines and the management of budget organization).
