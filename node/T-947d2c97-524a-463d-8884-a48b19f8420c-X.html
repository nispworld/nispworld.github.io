Title
Determine Concurrency Needs
Description

Concurrency is a shorthand expression to ensure that new developments do not outstrip the organisation’s ability to handle and support it. For a development to ‘be concurrent ‘ or ‘meet concurrency ‘ the organization must have enough capacity to serve each proposed development in a timely manner.
