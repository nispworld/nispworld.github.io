Title
PR Isolated Personnel Recovery Process
Description

The Personnel Recovery (PR) Isolated Personnel Recovery Process enables the coordinated actions and efforts of commanders and staffs, forces,and isolated personnel to bring isolated personnel under the physical custody of a friendly organization. The recovery task is accomplished through PR operation and mission planning, individual and synergistic actions of commanders and staffs, forces, and isolated personnel.
