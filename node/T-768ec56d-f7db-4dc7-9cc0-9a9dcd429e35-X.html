Title
AMD Air Defence Plan Development Processes
Description

The Air and Missile Defence (AMD) Air Defence Plan (ADP) Development Processes are used to compile and coordinate the ADP with component liaisons/air and missile defence commands, Host Nation, Allied and Partner Nation forces before it is submitted for approval by higher commands and authorities. The ADP is designed to be the ADC’s plan of action and is a ‘living’ document. Supporting forces may wish to provide supplements to the plan to refelect additional guidance or intentions. The ADP may serve as a reference document to other more detailed messages such as OPTASKLINK, OPTASKAAW, SPINS and ACO.
