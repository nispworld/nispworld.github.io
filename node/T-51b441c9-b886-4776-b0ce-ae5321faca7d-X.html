Title
Credential Management Applications
Description

The Credential Management Applications enable users to issue and manage a life-cycle of different types of credentials, bind issued credentials to recognized identities within shared Identity Repository and made issued credentials available for retrieval. The primary purpose of issued credentials is to support authentication of the users recognized within an enterprise.
