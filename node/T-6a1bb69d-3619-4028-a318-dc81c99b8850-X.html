Title
MILENG Contribution to MRO Development Process
Description

During the OPP Phase 3 at Strategic level Phase 3 Military Response Options (MROs) the RDG develops the MROs under an ACOS J5 lead, supported by the CCOMC and SHAPE directorates and specialist staff (as required and approved), for NAC consideration in support of their Political-Military Estimate (PME) process. As the force capability requirements are being generated for each MRO, the corresponding MILENG support requirements are also developed, together with the requirement for contractors, specialist capabilities and the likely needs of the Land, Maritime, Air and SOCC.
