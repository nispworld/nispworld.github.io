Title
Logistics
Description

Logistics information products are those that are used in, processed at and/or produced by the Logistics processes for command and control (C2) of a mission (the J-4 level of the Continental Staff System), such as information about ammunition, about petrol, oil and lubricants (POL), movement and transportation, about medical support and about host nation and multinational support.
