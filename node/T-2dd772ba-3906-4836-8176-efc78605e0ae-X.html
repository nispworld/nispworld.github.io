Title
ORBAT Management Application
Description

The Order of Battle (ORBAT) Management Application handles the Order of Battle according to MC 53/3, Terms of Reference for the Supreme Allied Commander Europe, after SACEUR is authorised to request periodic national order of battle reports in peacetime and accelerated order of battle reporting by national authorities during periods of crisis or conflict. The application supports the NATO ORBAT management process; this shall include creating and updating of ORBATs, the validation of the force data provided, and population of a common repository for (re-)use during Force Generation including sharing of the information with the COI. The application enables the use of new concepts and doctrine such as Defence Planning Capability Codes, in the creation and management of requirement templates and ORBATs.
