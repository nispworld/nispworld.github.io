Title
Spectrum Management Applications
Description

The Spectrum Management Applications enable users to functionally manage the (military) use of the electromagnetic spectrum, support the operational planning as well as near real-time management of radio frequency spectrum with emphasis on assigning compatible frequencies and performing spectrum engineering tasks. These applications support JTF electromagnetic spectrum management functions, supporting submission, coordination, and de-confliction of EMS requirements. It assists the theatre Interface Control Team in integrating and de-conflicting airborne network users.
