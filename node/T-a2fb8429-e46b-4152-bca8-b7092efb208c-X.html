Title
Financial Support Processes
Description
The processes here govern the financial administration of all civilian and military headquarters and other organizations established pursuant to the Alliance Treaty. The Heads of Alliance bodies are responsible and accountable for sound financial management and shall put in place the necessary governance arrangements to ensure and maintain this. This shall include, but is not limited to, the establishment and maintenance of financial governance, resource management practices, internal controls and financial information systems to achieve the efficient and effective use of resources. All Alliance staff, military and civilian, are obligated to comply with these processes, associated Financial Rules and Procedures and internal implementing directives.
