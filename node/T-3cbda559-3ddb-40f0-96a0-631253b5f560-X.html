Medical Evaluation Self-Assessments Review Process
Description

; Review Self-Assessments ‘UOR for a MEDICS Medical Evaluation Manual Capability, v1.0 (FINAL) ‘ The aim of this phase is for the MET to review the self-assessments provided by the TCNs and conduct an analysis using the background information provided by the TCNs and LN on personnel and equipment establishment, organisation and SOPs. QPTE form is also used as a supporting document in the review. Activities [MET Members/LE] *Check TCN Self-Assessments for minimum required completeness and inconsistencies.
