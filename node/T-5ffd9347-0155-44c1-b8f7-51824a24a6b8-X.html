Title
ACC OPP Course of Action Selection Process
Description

The Air Component Command (ACC) Operations Planning Process (OPP) Course of Action (COA) Selection Process supports the selection of a COA from which the COM JFAC will construct a Concept of Operations (CONOPS) The COM JFAC may select a COA, with or without modification, or may direct that additional COAs be investigated.
