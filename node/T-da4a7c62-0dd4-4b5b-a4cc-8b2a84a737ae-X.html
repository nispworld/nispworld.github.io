Title
Disseminate JISR Products
Description

The Joint Intelligence, Surveillance and Reconnaissance (JISR) Dissemination Process supports timely posting or publishing of exploited and processed information for use in follow on activities outside the JISR process (i.e. intelligence analysis, targeting). The JISR Dissemination Process involves the transmission of the collected exploited information to the requester. It may take on various forms real time or NRT data links directly from collection platform to requesters.
