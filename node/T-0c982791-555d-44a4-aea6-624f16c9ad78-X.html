Title
SMC Transition Planning and Support Process
Description

The Service Management and Control 9SMC) Transition Planning and Support Process is the business process responsible for planning all service transition processes and coordinating the resources to deploy a major release within the predicted cost, time and quality estimates. Occasionally Transition Planning and Support is referred to as ‘Project Management ‘.
