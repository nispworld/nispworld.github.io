Title
Frequency Engineering Application
Description

The Frequency Engineering Application enables users to perform point-to-point propagation and link analysis; to generate radio line-of-sight and received power, terrain-dependent coverage plots; to select display of terrain elevations, political boundaries, roads, waterways, towns, and stations in user-specified colors; to perform High Frequency (HF) propagation predictions; to calculate satellite look angles; to generate spectrum occupancy graphs; to perform intermodulation/harmonics analysis; to analyze band assignments, space assignments, and radars; and to perform geomagnetic conversions.
