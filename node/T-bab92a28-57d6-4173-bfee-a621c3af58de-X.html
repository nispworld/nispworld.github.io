Title
Manage Funding Contribution Adjustments
Description

Member governments may be requested to make an advance payment on the first contribution instalment for a financial year, in an amount sufficient to enable the financing of authorised expenditure required pending receipt of the first contribution instalment for the budget of that year. The amount of such an advance shall be added to the final contribution instalment for the preceding financial year as assessed. If a budget has not been approved, payment of advances on contribution instalments may be requested pending approval of the budget, up to the amounts of the provisional appropriations granted. All advances shall be assessed and requested and paid by and credited to member governments in the manner prescribed for normal contribution instalments.
