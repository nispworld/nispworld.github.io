Title
Perform Actor Evaluation Process
Description

The JIPOE Actor Evaluation Service supports identification of an actor’s doctrinal course of action independent of terrain and weather constraints, (i.e. how the actor fights according to his tactical doctrine or based on experience from previous operations). Actor evaluation consists of finding the actor; identifying the actor’s tactical doctrine or methods of operation; determining his doctrinal course of action; how the actor normally operates and how it has responded to similar situations in the past.
