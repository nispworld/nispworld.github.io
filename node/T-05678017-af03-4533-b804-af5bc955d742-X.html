Title
CIS
Description

CIS information products are those that are used in, processed at and/or produced by the Communications and information systems (CIS) operations and maintenance processes for command and control (C2) of a mission (the J-6 level of the Continental Staff System), such as information about communications plans, networks, frequencies and gateways, about numbering and addressing, about frequency and service management, and signals.
