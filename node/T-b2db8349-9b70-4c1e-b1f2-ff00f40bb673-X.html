Title
Reporting and Assessment Applications
Description

The Reporting and Assessment Applications enable users to use available information to conduct an assessment of current and planned operations. The applications also enable users to maintain and analyse Battle Damage Assessment (BDA) and target estimates to maintain the Situational Awareness of the Air campaign. Furthermore Reporting and Assessment Applications enable operational users to evaluate and record weapon performance as well as to conduct trend analysis to find deficiencies in weapon systems, munitions or tactics.
