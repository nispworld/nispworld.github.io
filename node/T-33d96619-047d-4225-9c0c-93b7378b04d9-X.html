Title
Portable Equipment
Description

Portable Equipment is a type of user equipment that can easily be moved from one place to another, that is intended to be moved while in operation and is generally powered by a rechargeable battery. Portable equipment typically is designed to perform a specific set of user functions such as computing, telecommunicating, reproducing, and presenting encoded information in myriad forms (i.e. playing music, photography, or editing text). Examples are smartphones and personal digital assistants (PDAs).
