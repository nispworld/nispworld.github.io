Title
Crisis Indication and Warning
Description

The Crisis Indications and Warning (I&W) Process involves the initial consideration of information on a particular issue that is of potential interest to the Government, that has come to light through the [[Indications and Warning]] I&W or other information provided by the intelligence community or other sources, to determine if further military action is required.
