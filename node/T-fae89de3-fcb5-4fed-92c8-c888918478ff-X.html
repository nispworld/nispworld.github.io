Title
Unified Communication Application
Description

The Unified Communication Application integrates all forms of digital communication and messaging, the channels used and the information types supported, into a consolidated, coherent and interoperable application. This provides users with a single-point-of-entry and consistent interface to facilitate more efficient and effective communication, collaboration and management of communication services, functionality and messaging between users across a digital network.
