Title
CIS Support Plan (SUPPLAN)
Description

The CIS SUPPLAN is the final definition of the required CIS support. It is approved by DCOS CCD and is developed by the CIS supporting entities with NCISG in lead and NCIA in support. It is based on the strategic and operational CIS guidance (CONOPS and OPLANs) and it contains all the details about how CIS support is going to be executed at the operational and tactical level.
