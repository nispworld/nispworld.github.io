Title
Record MEDINTEL Alerts and Observations Process
Description

The MEDINTEL Products, Alerts and Observations are stored in the MEDINTEL Library. This use case describes recording alerts and observations. ‘Preconditions ‘ A Disease Surveillance Capability exists and can provide data Direct observations of facilities have been carried out Information with medical interests exists in public and open source systems ‘Basic Flow ‘ 1. Maintain or import Alerts and Observations 2. Medical Support Staff contribute to Alerts and Observations ‘Alternative Flows ‘ Steps may be executed in any order ‘Post-conditions ‘ Alerts and Observations data has been maintained ‘Assumptions ‘ Authorised users can search, view, create and update alerts and observations that may be relevant to medical intelligence
