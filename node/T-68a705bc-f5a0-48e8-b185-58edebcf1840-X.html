Title
ASC Manage Airspace Requests Process
Description

The Airspace Control (ASC) Manage Airspace Requests Process enables the generation, submission and processing of Airspace Control Means Requests (ACMREQ). Based on operational requirements, subordinate and supporting Air, Land, and Maritime commanders forward their ACMREQs for the activation or deactivation of ACMs to the Joint Airspace Control Cell (JACC). The JACC generates the Airspace Control Order once all requests have been addressed and conflicts resolved.
