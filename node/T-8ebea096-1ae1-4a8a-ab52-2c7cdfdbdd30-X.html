Title
Maritime Control Processes
Description

In striking a balance between orchestrating operations and granting freedom of action to subordinates, commanders will use three methods of control mission command, command by veto, or detailed control type orders. The type of control used will depend on the nature of the operation or task, the environment, the nature and capabilities of the adversary and, perhaps most of all, the quality of the force and its respective commanders.
