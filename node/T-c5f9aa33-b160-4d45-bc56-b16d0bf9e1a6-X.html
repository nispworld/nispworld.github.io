Title
JISR Processing Applications
Description

The Joint Intelligence, Surveillance and Reconnaissance (JISR) Processing Applications enable users to contribute in that part of the intelligence cycle where the information which has been collected in response to the direction of the commander is converted into intelligence. Processing is a structured series of actions which, although set out sequentially, may also take place concurrently. It is defined as “The conversion of information into intelligence through collation, evaluation, analysis, integration and interpretation”.
