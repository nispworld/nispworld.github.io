Title
Recognized Environmental Picture Application
Description

The Recognized Environmental Picture (REP) Application enables users to access and manage the environmental picture as described by AAP-6 as the complete and seamless depiction of geospatial, oceanographic and meteorological information designated for the planning and conduct of joint operations in a specific area at a specific time and which supports the unity of effort across the joint Battlespace.
