Title
Maritime Amphibious Warfare Application
Description

The Maritime Amphibious Warfare Application enables users to plan the conduct of amphibious warfare that incorporates virtually all types of ships, submarines, aircraft, weapons, and Landing Forces (LFs) in a concerted military effort launched from the sea by a naval and landing force embarked in ships or craft.
