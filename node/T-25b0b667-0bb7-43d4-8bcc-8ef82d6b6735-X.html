Title
Experimentation
Description

Defense Experimentation is the application of the experimental method to the solution of complex defense capability development problems, potentially across the full spectrum of conflict types. Experimentation offers a unique means to support the development and transformation of military forces by advancing the knowledge of complex networked systems and capabilities and their potential for developing them further for future fielding.
