Title
Maritime Domain Services
Description
The Maritime Domain Services provide unique computing and information services in support of Maritime Operations. It supports the set of military activities that are conducted by maritime air, surface, sub-surface and amphibious forces to attain and maintain a desired degree of control of the surface, sub-surface, and air above the sea, influence events ashore, and, as required, support operations in the other operational domains.
