Title
Identity Card Printer
Description

The Identity Card Production Equipment is an electronic device that can produce plastic identity cards. Typically it prints user identity information (including user picture) on the card and digitally stores selected identity information (including digital certificates associated with the user) on either a barcode, magnetic strip, electronic chip or another storage medium embeded on the card.
