Title
Resource Testing Services
Description

The Resource Testing Services provides the necessary means to implement and enforce SMC resource testing policies at the communications level. Resource Testing Services will test specific resources to ensure they are operating within normal parameters. The objective is to verify whether the resources are working correctly and meet the appropriate performance levels.
