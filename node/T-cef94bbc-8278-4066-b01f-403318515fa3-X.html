Title
Browser Application
Description

The Browser Application enables users to retrieve, present and traverse information resources dispersed over a network. An information resource is identified by a Uniform Resource Identifier (URI) and may be a web page, image, video or other piece of content. Hyperlinks present in resources enable users easily to navigate their browsers to related resources.
