Title
Audit Management Application
Description

The Audit Management Application enables users to regularly and systematically review the application of CIS security during operation, which results from the collection of evidence regarding compliance to policy and risk management. It also enables the analysis of this evidence and the presentation of findings to the appropriate authorities for the purpose of accountability.
