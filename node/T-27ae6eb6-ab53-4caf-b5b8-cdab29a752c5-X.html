Title
Person of Maritime Interest Application
Description

A Maritime Person of Interest is anyone associated with maritime interests. These people might include owners, past and potential crew members, pirates (suspected or confirmed), dockworkers and passengers. The Person of Maritime Interest Application maintains lists of people related to maritime interests. There may be more than one list each having different identification.
