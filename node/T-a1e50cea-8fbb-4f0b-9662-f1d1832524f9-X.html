Title
Land Battle Space Management Deconfliction Application
Description

The Land Battle Space Management Deconfliction Application allows planners to plot and deconflict Battle Space Coordination Measures during the planning process. This application should also link directly to the Air and Maritime Battle Space Management Deconfliction Tool to allow Joint Battle Space Management deconfliction as required.
