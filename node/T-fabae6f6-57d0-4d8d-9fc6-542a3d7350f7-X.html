Title
Digital Camera
Description

A Digital Camera (or ‘digicam ‘) is a type of user equipment that is able to take video (DVC) or still photographs (DSC), or both, digitally by recording images via an electronic image sensor. Some have a GPS receiver built in, and can produce Geotagged photographs. Digital cameras are either stand alone devices or are often incorporated into other appliances ranging from PDAs, tablets and mobile phones to vehicles.
