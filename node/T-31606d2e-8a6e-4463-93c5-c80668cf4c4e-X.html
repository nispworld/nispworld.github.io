Title
Video Analysis Application
Description

The Video Analysis Application enables users to import, annotate with graphics and text overlays or descriptive text segments, manipulate (e.g. change contrast, sharpen), visualise geo-referenced video products in a geographic view, tag with metadata, in order to analyse and exploit video products and approve for publication.
