Title
Intelligence Requirement Management Services
Description

The Intelligence Requirement Management (IRM) Services provide the means to access and manage information requirements, related information objects, their status and relationship to decision making. These services will also provide the means to generate and consume Requests for Information (RFIs). The services will allow for the management and tracking of requirements, and related information objects, throughout the intelligence cycle (direction, collection, processing, analysis, dissemination and feedback).
