Title
CTE Missions Application
Description

The Collective Training and Exercises (CTE) Missions Application enables users to develop and manage missions in order to provide a coherent representation of military missions across all relevant applications involved in collective training and exercises. The CTE Missions Application unambiguously describes military plans at a relevant level of detail and for relevant force types and serves to enhance the communication between EXCON cells and simulation interfaces (human or otherwise).
