Title
Activity Management Process
Description

The Activity Management Process enables to create, capture and share knowledge inside Comunities of Interest. Knowledge is something which is not tangible (“An Organisation is managing People who have Knowledge but is not managing Knowledge”). Thus this has to be managed in a specific way in order to be acquired and maintained inside an Organisation;
