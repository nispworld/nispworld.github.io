Title
SMC Service Portfolio Management Process
Description

The Service Management and Control (SMC) Service Portfolio Management Process is the business process responsible for managing the service portfolio. Service Portfolio Management ensures that the service provider has the right mix of services to meet required business outcomes at an appropriate level of investment. Service Portfolio Management considers services in terms of the business value that they provide.
