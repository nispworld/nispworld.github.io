Title
SMC Facilities Management Process
Description

The Service Management and Control (SMC) Facilities Management Process is the business process that manages the physical environment where the IT infrastructure is located. Facilities Management includes all aspects of managing the physical environment, for example power and cooling, building access management, and environmental monitoring.
