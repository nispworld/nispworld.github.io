Title
JISR Collection Process
Description

The Joint Intelligence, Surveillance and Reconnaissance (JISR) Collection Process supports the gathering of unprocessed data. It can take the form of various collection disciplines such as imagery (IMINT), signal intelligence (SIGINT), or a report from a person (HUMINT). The JISR Collection Process comprises of Collection Operations Management and ISR Asset Collection.
