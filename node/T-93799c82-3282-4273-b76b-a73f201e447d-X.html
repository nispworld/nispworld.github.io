Title
Provide Medical Logistics Framework
Description

The medical logistic system needs to ensure the sustainability of the medical support system under all operational conditions. National responsibility over planning and executing an effective medical logistic system remains the guiding principle for operational support; however the Alliance Commander may exercise his authority to assure best possible coordination of national activities in this area. Economy of scale may benefit from coordinated supply to common funded multinational assets.
