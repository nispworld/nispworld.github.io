Title
Jane's Books Application
Description

The Jane’s Books Application enables users to access a series of publications that provide open source information and analysis about defence, security, transport, public safety and law enforcement. Jane’s products range from tri-service integrated resources to titles with a dedicated air, land or naval focus and are available by subscription.
