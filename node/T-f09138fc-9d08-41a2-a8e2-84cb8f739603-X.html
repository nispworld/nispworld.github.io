Title
Master Air Operations Planning Application
Description

The Master Air Operations Planning Application enables users to create a time-phased air and space scheme of manoeuvre for a given Air Tasking Order period in order to synthesize commander’s guidance, desired effects, and supported components’ schemes of manoeuvre, available resources, friendly capabilities, and enemy capabilities.
