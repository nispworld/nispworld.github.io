Title
Personnel and Administration
Description

Personnel and administration information products are those that are used in, processed at and/or produced by the personnel management and administrative support processes for command and control (C2) of a mission (the J-1 level of the Continental Staff System), such as human resources, personnel and manpower information as well as administrative, secretarial and organizational information.
