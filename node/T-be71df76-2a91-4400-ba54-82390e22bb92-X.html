Title
Antenna
Description

A radio antenna is an electrical device which converts electric currents into radio waves, and vice versa. Antennas are essential components of all equipment that uses radio and are usually used with a radio transmitter or radio receiver. Antennas can also be used as sensor equipment. According to their applications and technology available, antennas generally fall in one of two categories
