Title
IED Incident Exploitation Application
Description

The Improvised Explosive Device (IED) Incident Exploitation Application enables users to access and update intelligence reports regarding IED events, insurgents, persons, equipment, casualties and locations that could have significant effect on current planning and operations or information that may be of interest at the alliance, coalition or national level in order to support the counter IED activities, which include the following three pillars Attack the Network, Defeat the Device, and Prepare the Force.
