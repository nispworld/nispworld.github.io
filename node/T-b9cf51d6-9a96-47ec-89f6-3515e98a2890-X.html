Title
Manage Funding Contributions
Description

Payments in accordance with budget appropriations shall be financed by contributions from member governments according to the cost-sharing formula agreed by the appropriate governing body in force for the financial year concerned. Expenditure against a committed appropriation carried forward shall be shared in accordance with the cost-sharing formula applicable to the budget code and budget year against which the commitment was initially made.
