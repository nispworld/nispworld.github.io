Title
Air Strategy Development Processes
Description
The Air Strategy Development Processes encompass all efforts to develop situational awareness, provide assessments and advice, analyze missions, develop Concepts of Operations (CONOPS), and develop Joint Air Operation Plans, all as part of the [[Plan Operations Operations Planning Process]] (OPP). The Air Component Command (ACC) typically leads the Air Strategy Development Processes.
