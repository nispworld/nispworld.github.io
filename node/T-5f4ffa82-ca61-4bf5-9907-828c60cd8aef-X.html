Title
AMD Air Defence Mission Analysis and Statement Processes
Description

The Air and Missile Defence (AMD) Air Defence Mission Analysis and Statement Processes consist of business processes for updating the Prioritised Critical Asset List (PCAL), developing the Joint Prioritised Defended Asset List (JPDAL), examining commanders guidance and other source planning documents, and creating the ‘shell ‘ for the Air Defence Plan (ADP).
