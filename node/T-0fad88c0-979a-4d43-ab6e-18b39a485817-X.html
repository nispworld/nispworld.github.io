Title
Analytics Application
Description

The Analytics Application enables users to identify, extract and analyze data from a variety of data repositories and typically provide historical, current and predictive views of relevant operations supporting decision-making. Common functions of of Analytics Application are reporting; online analytical processing (OLAP); analytics; data mining; business performance management; benchmarking; and predictive analytics.
