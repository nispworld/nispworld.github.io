Title
Intelligence
Description

Intelligence information products are those that are used in, processed at and/or produced by the intelligence, surveillance and reconnaissance (ISR) processes for command and control (C2) of a mission (the J-2 level of the Continental Staff System), such as Information Requirement Management (IRM), Collection Management (CM), products for intelligence analysis, collection, direction, dissemination and exploitation, and related requests, reports and summaries.
