Title
SMC Service Transition Processes
Description

The Service Management and Control (SMC) Service Transition Processes are the business processes that ensures that new, modified or retired services meet the expectations of the business as documented in the service strategy and service design stages of the lifecycle. Although these processes are associated with Service Transition, most processes have activities that take place across multiple stages of the service lifecycle.
