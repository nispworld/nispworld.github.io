Title
PR Isolated Personnel Support Process
Description

The Personnel Recovery (PR) Isolated Personnel Support Process is employed by the Joint Personnel Recovery Centre (JPRC) and the Personnel Recovery Coordination Centre (PRCC) maintaining situational awareness and ensuring the sustainment of the Isolated Personnel (ISOP). Support to the ISOP may include establishment of two-way communications, provising morale building support, aerial resupply, suppression of enemy threats or other means to preclude capture or a worsening situation.
