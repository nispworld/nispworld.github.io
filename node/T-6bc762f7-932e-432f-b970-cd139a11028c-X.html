Title
TST Targeting Process
Description

The Time Sensitive Targeting (TST) Attack Operations Targeting Process satisfies restrictions including CDE, ROE, restricted/prohibited targets of the JTL or JPTL, and deconfliction. This process aligns engagement capabilities with the desired effect, completes a risk assessment, and determines required force packaging. Final approval for TST engagement is made during the target step, and the output is a tasking to the selected component engagement system.
