Title
Maritime Warfare Area Applications
Description

The Maritime Warfare Area Applications enable users to support the activities conducted by maritime air, surface, subsurface and amphibious forces to attain and maintain a desired degree of control of the surface, subsurface, and air above the sea, influence events ashore, and, as required, support land, air operations and space operations.
