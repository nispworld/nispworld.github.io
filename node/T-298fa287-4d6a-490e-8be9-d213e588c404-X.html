Title
Business Rules Services
Description

The Business Rules Services provide capability to support the creation, testing, management, deployment and maintenance of Business Rules in an operational environment. A Business Rules are statements describing a business/enterprise policy or procedure (e.g. discount calculation) and can be represented using formal language.
