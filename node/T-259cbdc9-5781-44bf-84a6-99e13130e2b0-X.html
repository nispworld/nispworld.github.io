Title
Space Support Coordination During Operational Execution
Description

During the execution phase of a Alliance operation, there will be a need for managing, coordinating and adjusting space support. The space coordination function at each level provides feedback on operational execution and helps identify any emerging space support requirements which can be fulfilled using formal arrangements and the OPLAN ANNEX DD. This process supports the battle staff across all functional areas by marrying available Space capabilities to support dynamic operational requirements. This may result in updating space related documents (OPLAN ANNEX DD, FRAGOs, TTPs, and SOPs) as required.
