Title
ACC OPP Situational Awareness Development Process
Description

The Air Component Command (ACC) Operations Planning Process (OPP) Situational Awareness Development Process is employed in advance of an alliance response to a crisis and continues in support of all subsequent phases. The purpose of this process is to develop and maintain a level of knowledge to enable air assessments and decision making to support the development of COM AIRCOM/COM JFAC advice to the Strategic Commander/COM JFC during during the planning phase and throughout an operation. This phase will often be initiated under the responsibility of the Peacetime Establishment (PE) staff.
