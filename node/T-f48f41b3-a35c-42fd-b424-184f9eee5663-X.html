Title
CTE Geospatial Application
Description

The Collective Training and Exercises (CTE) Geospatial Application enables users to develop and manage fictitious geospatial source information and to generate specific geospatial data products in order to provide coherent representation of geospatial elements across all relevant applications involved in collective training and exercise events.
