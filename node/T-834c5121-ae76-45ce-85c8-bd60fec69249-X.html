Title
Air Applications
Description
The Air Applications enable users to collect, process, present and distribute information that supports the major functions of air operations. Air Operations are the set of military activities that are conducted by air forces to attain and maintain a desired degree of control of the air, influence events on land and along coastal areas, and, as required, support operations in the other operational domains.
