Title
CBRN Casualty Rate Estimation Application
Description

The Chemical, Biological, Radiological and Nuclear (CBRN) Casualty Rate Estimation (CRE) Application enables users to obtain the casualty rate estimates for CBRN cases. The CBRN CRE capability applies to all NATO operations and exercises and provides an analytical tool for all personnel involved in operational planning i.e., personnel, operational and medical staffs.
