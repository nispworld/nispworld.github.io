Title
Maintain Strategic Situational Awareness
Description

The Government must have adequate, sufficient and flexible capabilities to respond to current and future challenges. Through its [[Defence Planning]] Process, the Defence Enterprise identifies the capabilities and forces it requires. Strategic Situational Awareness is the ability to characterize the current and future operating environment including adversary capabilities and distribution of forces, detect attacks, and discern actual attacks from false alarms across the spectrum of conflict, both conventional and nuclear.
