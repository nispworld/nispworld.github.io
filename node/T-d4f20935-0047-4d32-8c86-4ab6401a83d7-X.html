Title
Strategic Communications Processes
Description

Strategic Communications Processes assist the coordinated and appropriate use of alliance communications activities and capabilities on behalf of the alliance policies, operations and activities, and in order to advance the alliance’s aims. The aim of alliance Strategic Communications (Stratcom) is to ensure that audiences whether in the partner nations or in a region where an alliance operation is taking place, either friendly or adversarial, receives truthful, accurate and timely information that will allow them to understand and assess the Alliance’s actions and intentions. The list of associated disciplines includes Public Diplomacy, Public Affairs (PA), Military Public Affairs, Information Operations (Info Ops), Psychological Operations (PSYOPS).
