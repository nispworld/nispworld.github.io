Title
Military Engineering Information Management Application
Description

The Military Engineering Information Management Application supports the planning and execution of Military Engineering (MILENG) combat and force support at strategic, operational and tactical level with ability to access a large amount of classified and unclassified information. This information must be able to be managed, updated, searched, collated, analysed and shared throughout the engineer community and available for other user communities in both classified and unclassified environment.
