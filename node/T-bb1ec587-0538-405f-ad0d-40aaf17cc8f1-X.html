Title
Space Domain Awareness Services
Description

The Space Domain Awareness (SDA) Services provide the means to build, maintain and share a common understanding of the Space environment including threats and vulnerabilities that have the potential to affect NATO operations, missions and functions. They include, but are not limited to, means to elaborate and share the Recognized Space Picture (RSpP).
