Title
CTE Event Planning and Management Applications
Description

The Collective Training and Exercises (CTE) Event Planning and Management Applications enable users to handle the event-related aspects of group training and exercise events in order to provide the training audience with a focussed and coherent venue to meet their objectives. Event-related aspects include estimate and plan, schedule, control cost and manage budget, allocate resources, collaborate, communicate, make decisions, manage quality, and document or administer.
