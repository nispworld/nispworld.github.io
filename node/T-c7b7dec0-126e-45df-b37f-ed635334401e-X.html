Title
Financial Management Process
Description

The Financial Management Process is the business process responsible for managing appropriated funds, budgeting, accounting and charging requirements. Financial Management for managing Alliance funds requires an appropriate level of funding to design, develop and deliver services that meet the strategy of the organization in a cost-effective manner.
