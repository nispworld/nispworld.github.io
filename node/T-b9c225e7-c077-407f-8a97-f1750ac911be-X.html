Title
Service Inventory Management Application
Description

The Service Inventory Management Application enables users to maintain information about the instances of services in an organisation. The Service Inventory Management Application is used to store and manage information about Customer facing service instances and their attributes, and Resource facing service instances and their attributes. The Service Inventory Management Application is also used to store and manage information about the mapping of services to other services and/or resources used to implement the service.
