Title
Information Discovery Services
Description
The Information Discovery Services provide the functionality to automate the discovery and retrieval of Information Products and their structure. Information Products, in this regard, are aggregates of structured data. Discovered data is the result of a search upon an entire dataset, a search upon a subset of a dataset, or a search based on dataset and/or content metadata.
