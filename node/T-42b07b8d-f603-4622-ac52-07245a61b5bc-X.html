Title
Request External Financial Audit
Description

External audit of the Alliance bodies subject to Established Regulations shall be performed by a Board of Auditors appointed by the Council. The Board, constituted as the International Board of Auditors (IBA), shall operate in accordance with the Charter approved by the Alliance Council. The Council alone shall have the authority to amend the IBA Charter, based on advice and recommendations from the IBA and the Resource Policy and Planning Board.
