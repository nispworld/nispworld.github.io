Title
CBRN Force Preparation Application
Description

The Chemical, Biological, Radiological and Nuclear (CBRN) Force Preparation Application enables users to facilitate the Force Preparation for CBRN operations or NATO operations under CBRN conditions. The application enables users to familiarize with the CBRN Functional Services and apply it also for training and exercise (training mode).
