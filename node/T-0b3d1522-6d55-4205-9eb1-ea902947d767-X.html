Title
Air Intercept Mission Control Application
Description

The Air Intercept Mission Control Application enables users to guide missions and to perform ground controlled interception. This application also provides mission assistance for advisory information and less structured guidance. This application will enable the operator to perform handovers (transfer of control) between controlling units (military or civilian) and support recovery assistance including air-to air refuelling, emergency assistance, Combat Search and Rescue (CSAR), and Safe Passage coordination requests.
