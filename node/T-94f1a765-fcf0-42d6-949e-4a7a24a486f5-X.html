Title
CIS Processes
Description

The processes for Communications and Information Systems (CIS) describe the activities to provide services and functions for the proper conduct of command and control (C2) and for effective information sharing. Together with standardized procedures and information management principles, CIS forms a system that the commander, staff, and subordinates use to plan, direct, coordinate, and control operations.
