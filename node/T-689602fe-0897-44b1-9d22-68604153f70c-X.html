Title
Intelligence Collection Process
Description

The Intelligence Data Collection Process supports the exploitation of sources by collection agencies and the delivery of the information obtained to the appropriate processing unit for use in the production of intelligence. Intelligence, Surveillance and Reconnaissance (ISR) assets conduct the bulk of all collection activities, but non-dedicated ISR assets can also contribute. Collection activity requires close collaboration with both intelligence and command staff to optimize the use of collection assets.
