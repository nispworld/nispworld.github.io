Title
AMD Defence Design Generation Process
Description

The Air and Missile Defence (AMD) Defence Design Generation Process is employed to support defensive planning in support of Air and Missile Defence Plan (ADP) development. The defence design is focused on a layered defence building from the protected assets outward. The process may include developing the Regions/Sectors first and the placement of assets to defend, then the defenders, air orbits and CAP points etc. Command arrangements for air and missile defence ar often complex,
