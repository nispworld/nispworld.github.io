Title
CBRN Plans Development Application
Description

The Chemical, Biological, Radiological and Nuclear (CBRN) Plans Development Application enables users to exchange, share and coordinate information (based on word-processing, database, spreadsheet, image and presentational applications and support planning tools) to accumulate operational planning data and develop CBRN relevant concepts and plan (annexes to OPLAN/OPORD). It will also enable to manage (create, update, delete) and provide CBRN estimates during the planning process.
