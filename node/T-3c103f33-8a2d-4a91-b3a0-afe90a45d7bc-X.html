Title
CBRN Warning and Reporting Application
Description

The Chemical, Biological, Radiological and Nuclear (CBRN) Warning and Reporting Application enables users to aid the rapid collection, evaluation and dissemination of data concerning NBC attacks and hazards, including the prediction of hazard areas. The dissemination of data and the prediction of hazards is carried out using CIS.
