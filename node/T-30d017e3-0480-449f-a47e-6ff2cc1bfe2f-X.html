Title
Management of Non-Classified NATO Information
Description

The Management of Non-Classified NATO Information document is concerned with the protection and handling of non-classified NATO information. It establishes, in support of the NATO Information Management Policy, the basic principles and minimum standards to be applied by NATO civil and military bodies and by NATO member nations for the protection and handling of such non-classified NATO information. It complements NATO Security Policy, which establishes the basic principles and standards to be applied within NATO civil and military bodies and NATO member nations for the protection of NATO classified information.
