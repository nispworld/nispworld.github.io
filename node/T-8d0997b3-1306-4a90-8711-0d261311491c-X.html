Title
CTE MEL/MIL Application
Description

The Collective Training and Exercises (CTE) Main Event List (MEL) / Main Incident List (MIL) Application enables users to develop, manage and execute the scripting of a scenario captured in the MEL/MIL in order to provide input to the training audience based on a deliberate approach to achieving training objectives for collective training and exercises events.
