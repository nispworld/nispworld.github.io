Title
Prioritising Defence Industrial Development
Description
Aligning own capability priorities with those of international partners is challenging but can leverage synergies and result in long term cost savings. Prioritising National Defence Industrial Development should be conducted as a collaborative process of ensuring a transparent, cooperative and cost-effective approach to meet essential enterprise capability requirements, without compromising national interests.
