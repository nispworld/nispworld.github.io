Title
SMC Release and Deployment Management Process
Description

The Service Management and Control (SMC) Release and Deployment Management Process is the business process that is responsible for planning, scheduling and controlling the build, test and deployment of releases, and for delivering new functionality required by the business while protecting the integrity of existing services. The primary goal of Release Management is to ensure that the integrity of the live environment is protected and that the correct components are released.
