Title
CIS Support Processes
Description
The CIS Support Process are composed of a collection of business processes that are implemented and executed to support the coherent management of components in a service-enabled Communications and Information Systems (CIS) environment. CIS Support Processes also support of the security objectives that are implemented and executed to guarantee adequate levels of confidentiality, integrity and availability of information.
