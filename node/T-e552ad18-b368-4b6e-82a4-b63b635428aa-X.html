Title
Personnel Recovery C2 Application
Description

The Personnel Recovery Command and Control (C2) Application enables users to create a collaborative environment to assist personnel assigned to the Joint Personnel Recovery Center (JPRC) and the Personnel Recovery Coordination Cell (PRCC) in reporting, locating, supporting, recovering and reintegrating isolated personnel throughout the full spectrum of NATO operations.
