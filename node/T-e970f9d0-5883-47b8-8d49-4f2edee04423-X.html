Title
Capability Development Initiation Processes
Description
The processes for Capability Development Initiation are the first phase in the Capability Development processes. The main objectives are to help define the scope and benefit expectations and to ensure that authorization and initiation of Capability Development are linked to the organization’s ongoing work and strategic priorities. The initiation phase lays the foundation for Capability Development in five distinct ways
