Title
POL-MIL Consultation Application
Description

The POL-MIL Consultation Application assist in the preparation of products to support the political-military consultation process between the strategic military headquarters and the political leadership. The POL-MIL Consultation App fuses information from different services and applications, into products supporting the POL-MIL consultation process.
