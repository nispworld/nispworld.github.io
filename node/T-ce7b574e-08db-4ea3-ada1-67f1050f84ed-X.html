Title
Management of Estate, Buildings and Infrastructure Application
Description

The Management of Estate, Buildings and Infrastructure Application enables users to identify, assess and analyze infrastructure requirements and availability. The Management of Estate, Buildings and Infrastructure Application will allow the Joint Force Engineer Staff (JFEng) to implement the planning, maintenance and disposal of the infrastructure works required to support logistic mission and the survival of the Joint Force as a whole, in accordance with procedures laid down in AJP-3.12, Allied Joint Engineering Doctrine.
