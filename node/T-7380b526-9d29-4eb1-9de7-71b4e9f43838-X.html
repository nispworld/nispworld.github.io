Title
Assess the Security Environment
Description

Assessing all forces, entities, and systems that are external to an organization but can affect its operation. Analyze far-reaching currents in the macro situation, assess the competition, evaluate technological changes, and identify societal as well as ecological issues of concern. Create a big-picture understanding of externalities, with sufficient depth across individual aspects.
