Title
Directing
Description
The commander directs actions to ensure that current orders and directives are completed as intended. This direction is done with the broader purpose of accomplishing the overall mission. Tools like the commander’s intent and CCIRs assist the commander in this role. The preponderance of the commander’s directing function is conducted by the joint operations centre (JOC). Command styles are dependent on personality, and there is no single formula or template. In deciding on their approach to directing the joint force during execution, the commander should consider how best to utilise the following activities.
