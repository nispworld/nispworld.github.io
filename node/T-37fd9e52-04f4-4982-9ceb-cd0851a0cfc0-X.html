Title
Training and Exercises
Description

Training and exercises information products are those that are used in, processed at and/or produced by the education, training, exercises and evaluation (ETEE) processes for command and control (C2) of a mission (the J-7 level of the Continental Staff System), such as information about collective training and exercises (CTE), individual training, and training requirements, objectives, plans and reports.
