Title
Electromagnetic Operations Planning Application
Description

The Electromagnetic Operations (EMO) Planning Application enables the user to assess EW threat, plan best response, coordinate force deployment and communicate operational planning, with an Electronic Attack Plan, Electronic Defence Plan or Electronic Surveillance Plan. It will provide tasking orders such as ATO, SPINS, FRAGO, EWRTM, relating to EW, and the OPTASK EW.
