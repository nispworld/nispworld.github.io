Title
Intelligence Requirements Management Process
Description

Intelligence Requirements Management (IRM) is a set of integrated management processes and services which validate, summarize and prioritize incoming intelligence requirements; initiates the collection of associated information; quality controls processed outputs; and oversees dissemination of intelligence products. This management process is led by the intelligence staff or agency and provides the means to access and manage intelligence requirements, related information objects, their status and relationship to decision making. IRM underpins the vast majority of the intelligence cycle, ensuring that requirements are prioritized, coordinated and actioned correctly.
