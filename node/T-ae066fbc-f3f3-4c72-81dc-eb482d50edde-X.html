Title
Merchant Ship Characteristics Application
Description

Merchant ship characteristics, including images can be retrieved from external sources such as the Office of Naval Intelligence Database (U.S.) or Marine Traffic. The Merchant Ship Characteristics Application maintains a list of addresses to such databases, and allows access to them to validate merchant shipping data from unverified and external sources and to investigate vessels.
