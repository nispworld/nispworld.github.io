Title
Intelligence Reports Application
Description

The Intelligence Reports Application enables users to create, update, validate and approve Intelligence Reports according to report type specific templates in order to provide timely intelligence reports regarding equipment, events, organisations, persons, places, targets and units that could have significant effect on current planning and operations or information that may be of timely interest at the alliance, coalition or national level. Intelligence reports may be provided as a consequence of exploitation/analysis tasking or requests.
