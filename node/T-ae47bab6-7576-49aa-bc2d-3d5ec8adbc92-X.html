Title
Civil-Military Cooperation
Description

Civil-military cooperation (CIMC) information products are those that are used in, processed at and/or produced by the CIMIC processes for command and control (C2) or a mission (the J-9 level of the Continental Staff System), such as public information, civil-military information sharing (CMIS) and information about CIMIC plans and situation reports.
