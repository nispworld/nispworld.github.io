Title
Land OPLAN Review Process
Description

The Land Operational Plan (OPLAN) Review Process is taking place in the Execution, Assessment and OPLAN Review Processes (Phase 5 of Operations). The purpose of the Land OPLAN Review Process is to review the OPLAN in order to fit to the actual situation. This process is necessary for lead a land operation. This process is concomitant with the Land Execution processes and with the Land Assessment processes.
